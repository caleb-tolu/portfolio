This project is mainly focused on designing a tour application that offers the best user experience while booking their holiday trips, to help travellers find places & trips that match the most with their preferences, by providing as narrowed-down suggestions as possible. This application aims at making users customise their choices of book trips and hotels, also users can plan for their next trips in advance, meet friends even while on their trips. This app also will help users not have to compromise with their preferences/factors and skip the tedious task of researching on the internet.
